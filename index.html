Blocking Help

  • Instruct on G.O.O.D (and other blocking calls).

The Blocking Call has two parts, the Assignment and the Playside. The Assignment tells you who to block. The Playside tells you which direction to block.

The Assignment is an acronym, like G.O.O.D. or G.O.L.D. Each letter in the acronym represents an assignment to check - G for Gap (inside), O for On Me, etc...

  • G.O. is always the base
  • G. is always INSIDE gap (closest to the ball)
  • Color Coding (more fun than teaching)
